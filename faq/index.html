Snake Game FAQ

Clear answers about Snake rules, controls, scores, modes, mobile play, privacy and history. Snake 3D is free, works without an account, and stores personal records locally rather than in an online ranking.

Play Snake online
What is Snake?

Snake is a classic arcade game where the player guides a moving line or snake, collects food, grows longer, and avoids collisions. The challenge increases because every pickup reduces the free space available for future movement.

How do I play Snake?

Choose a direction, collect fruit, and avoid the snake body and solid obstacles. Use arrow keys or WASD on desktop, or swipe and touch arrows on mobile. Read the full beginner guide.

Is Snake 3D free?

Yes. Snake 3D is free to play in a supported web browser.

Do I need to download Snake 3D?

No. The game runs in the browser without an app download or account.

Can I play Snake on mobile?

Yes. Snake 3D supports swipe gestures and adjustable on-screen arrow controls on touch devices. See the mobile guide.

What are the Snake controls?

Use arrow keys or WASD on desktop. Use swipe gestures or the on-screen arrows on mobile. Escape or P pauses keyboard play. The complete mapping is on the controls page.

Can I pause Snake?

Yes. Press Escape or P on desktop, or use the pause button on touch devices. The browser also pauses an active run when the page becomes hidden.

Does Snake 3D save my best score?

Yes. The personal best is saved locally in the current browser and device.

Does Snake 3D have an online leaderboard?

No. There is currently no backend or public leaderboard. Scores remain local to the browser.

Does Snake 3D have levels?

Yes. Levels mode contains ten maps with different goals, obstacles, portal positions, and speeds.

What is Endless mode?

Endless mode continues until a collision. The snake grows and the pace becomes faster as the run progresses. It is the main mode for personal best scores.

How do portals work?

Entering one portal moves the snake head to its paired exit while preserving the direction of travel. Check the exit before entering because it can be near a wall or body segment.

What is the combo multiplier?

Collecting fruit quickly builds a multiplier that increases scoring. Waiting too long lets the combo reset. A safe route is more valuable than preserving a risky combo.

Why can I not reverse direction?

A direct reversal would move the head into the first body segment, so the game ignores that input. Turn perpendicular first.

Can I play Snake offline?

Snake 3D is a web game and should be loaded with an internet connection. It does not currently install an offline service worker.

Can I play Snake at school?

The game requires no installation, but access depends on the school device and network policies. It does not bypass restrictions.

Does Snake work on a Chromebook?

Yes, on a Chromebook with a modern supported browser. Keyboard and touch controls can both work on touchscreen models.

What happens at maximum Snake length?

There is no single maximum shared by every mode because maps have different free cells and obstacles. A run becomes increasingly constrained as the board fills.

Who invented Snake?

Snake developed as a game family rather than a single modern title invented at one moment. The 1976 arcade game Blockade is commonly treated as an early ancestor. Learn more in the history guide.

Who made Snake for Nokia phones?

Nokia design engineer Taneli Armanto programmed the version introduced on Nokia devices in 1997.

Is Snake 3D the same as Google Snake?

No. Snake 3D is an independent browser game with its own 3D board, portals, levels, scoring, and controls.

Can I turn off sound?

Yes. Open Settings and set Sound to Off. The preference is saved locally.

Can I lower graphics quality?

Yes. Settings offers Low, Medium, and High graphics quality. Lower quality can improve smoothness on older mobile hardware.

What data does Snake 3D save?

The browser stores the personal best and preferences such as language, quality, sound, and control size. There is no player profile.

How does the second life work?

After an Endless run, sharing the score on X can unlock one second-life attempt. The snake keeps its length and speed but restarts near the center after a countdown.

Which browsers support Snake 3D?

Snake 3D targets current versions of major browsers with WebGL and modern JavaScript support. If the board does not render, update the browser and confirm that hardware acceleration is available.
